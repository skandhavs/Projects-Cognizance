The Ocean






Covering 70 percent of the Earth's surface, the ocean transports heat from the equator to the poles, regulating our climate and weather patterns.

At least 14 million tons of plastic end up in the ocean every year,threatening and worsening climate change

The need for Ocean conservation is now paramount.
